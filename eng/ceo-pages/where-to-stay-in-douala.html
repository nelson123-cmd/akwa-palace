📍 Best Areas to Stay in Douala

The best area to stay in Douala is Akwa, the vibrant city center. This district offers upscale hotels, restaurants, nightlife, and is closest to the business hub and airport.

  • Akwa District – Central, safe, luxury hotels, embassies, and nightlife
  • Bonanjo – Administrative area, quiet, close to Akwa Palace
  • Bastos (Yaoundé reference) – Diplomatic quarter with high security
  • Douala Airport Area – Convenient for transit stays

✅ Why Akwa? Walking distance to restaurants, banks, shopping malls, and the famous Marché des Fleurs. Most tourists and business travelers choose Akwa for safety and convenience.

🛡️ Safety Guarantee: Akwa district has 24/7 tourist police patrols. All major hotels, including Akwa Palace, provide private security, CCTV, and secure transfers.
