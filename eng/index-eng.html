Welcome to Hôtel
Akwa Palace Douala

A warm atmosphere and first-class services in the centre of Douala

Established since 1951 in the heart of Douala, Cameroon’s economic capital, Akwa Palace Douala is a 4-star hotel renowned for its timeless elegance, distinctive cuisine and exceptional service. It offers 185 spacious, comfortable and modern rooms and suites for business travellers and tourists from all over the world.

The hotel offers a wide range of services including an outdoor swimming pool, a modern fitness centre, a relaxing spa and several restaurants. Its central location in the Akwa neighbourhood, close to the airport, administrative offices, shopping centres and tourist attractions, makes it an ideal choice for working and exploring the city of Douala.

The establishment also offers companies and individuals fully-equipped rooms for business events (meetings, seminars, conferences) and private functions (receptions, weddings, and birthdays among others).
