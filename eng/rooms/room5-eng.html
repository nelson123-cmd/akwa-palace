Senior Suite

Luxury suite in Douala

prev next

The Senior Suite at Hôtel Akwa Palace Douala is a haven of elegance and sophistication, offering a generous 54m² of living space. With its vintage style, this suite blends old-world charm with modern comforts, creating a refined, timeless atmosphere. It comprises a bedroom, a separate living room, a majestic bathroom and a guest shower. The private balcony lets you fully enjoy views of the Wouri River, the swimming pool or the gardens.

 

The Senior Suite is designed for those seeking elegance and luxury in an authentic and historic setting in Douala.
